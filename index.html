Workspace Tasks

A gentle brake before you close a workspace.

Most tab managers close everything and ask questions never. Tabspace lets you attach lightweight tasks to each workspace — so when you close a group with open items, it asks what you want to do instead of just disappearing.

Per-workspace tasks

Add tasks directly on a workspace card. They stay attached to that domain group across sessions.

Inline editing

Click to toggle done. Tasks live on the card itself — no separate app, no extra context switch.

Close dialog

Closing a workspace with open tasks surfaces a modal: finish one, defer them, or close anyway. Your call.

Reader Assist

When an article matters, make the page quieter.

Reader Assist adds focus controls to ordinary webpages: move through reading blocks, highlight selected text, save local annotations, and start a 15-minute reading timer.

Local-first

Your tabs and notes stay in your browser.

Tabspace does not send your tabs, bookmarks, notes, annotations, browsing activity, or webpage content to a remote server.

Reading queue items, extension settings, and Reader Assist annotations use Chrome local extension storage.

No ads. No resale of user data. No analytics pipeline for your browsing history.
