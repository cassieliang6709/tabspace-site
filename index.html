Local-first

Your tabs and notes stay in your browser.

Tabspace does not send your tabs, bookmarks, notes, annotations, browsing activity, or webpage content to a remote server.

Reading queue items, extension settings, and Reader Assist annotations use Chrome local extension storage.

No ads. No resale of user data. No analytics pipeline for your browsing history.
